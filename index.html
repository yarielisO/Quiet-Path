The Quiet Path

You've been walking for a while now.

The world feels slow today. The wind moves gently through the grass and the sky stretches endlessly above you. For once, there is nowhere you need to be and nothing demanding your attention.

Ahead of you, the path divides.

One road rises toward the distant mountains, the quiet and still beneath the light. The other descends toward the shoreline, where the sound of water moves softly through the air.

Both paths seem to be asking something of you.

You pause for a moment and choose where to go next.

Walk toward the mountains

Walk toward the shore
