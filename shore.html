The Shoreline

You follow the path down toward the shore.

The sound of the waves grows clearer as you walk. By the time you reach the sand, the water is moving in a steady rhythm, calm and endless. A few distant voices drift through the air, but the beach still feels peaceful, almost private.

As you walk along the edge of the water, something catches your eye.

Half buries in the sand is a small glass bottle. Inside, there seems to be a folded note.

You stop and look at it for a moment.

Open the bottle

Leave it and continue walking
