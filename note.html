The Note in the Bottle

You kneel down and brush the sand away from the bottle.

Inside, the paper is still dry. You carefully pull it out and unfold it.

It reads:

"Whoever finds this comma. I hope you are enjoying the moment you're in."

You smile without meaning to.

The message is simple, but it lands somewhere deep. A stranger, at some unknown time, paused, long enough to leave behind a reminder that feels meant for you now.
