The Carved Message

You step closer to the stone and run your eyes over the carved words.

"Pay attention to the small things. They are the only things that are truly happening."

You stay there for a while, letting the words settle into you.

The wind moves through the grass. Light stretches across the mountains. Nothing in the world has changed, and yet something in you feels quieter now.

Maybe wisdom is not always about finding something new. Maybe sometimes it is simply about noticing what was already there.
